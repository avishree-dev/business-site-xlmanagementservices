XL Management Services — Experience. Integrity. Performance.

Founded in Kolkata, XL Management Services has grown into a trusted recovery and portfolio management partner for banks, NBFCs, and financial institutions across Eastern India. For more than two decades, we have built our reputation on professionalism, accountability, and a commitment to delivering consistent results.

What began as a focused recovery operation has evolved into a structured organization supported by experienced leadership, dedicated field teams, and modern operational infrastructure. Today, we manage large recovery portfolios while maintaining the highest standards of compliance, transparency, and borrower engagement.

Our success is driven by people, processes, and long-term relationships. By combining industry expertise with ethical recovery practices, we help clients achieve stronger portfolio performance while protecting customer trust.

Managing portfolios for ICICI Bank, Axis Finance, SBI Cards, IDBI Bank, Bandhan Bank, and InCred Financial Services.
